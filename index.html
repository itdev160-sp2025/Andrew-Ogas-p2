Maze Runner Instructions

The goal of Maze Runner is to get to the end of the maze as fast as possible. Select the maze you want to run from the choices at the bottom. Then, click on the green box to start the maze.

Race to get to the red square as fast as possible. If you touch a black square or go out of bounds, you lose.

If you get a top five score, you will be able to save it on the leader board.
